Magic cake

Magic cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known.Few have tried it and lived to tell about it,and there are stories.
we also need to stress taht whatever happens to you as a result of consuming Magiccake is your responsibility.You have been warned,and you can view our legalpage for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar,and butter in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hr
  4. Lets si at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy
